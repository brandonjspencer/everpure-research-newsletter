Weekly Product Design Cycle

ZURB provides weekly concept testing, additional feature support for the experience and functionality, and web analysis.
There are three areas of consideration and work.
Weekly concept testing to improve the website. The testing will explore 2-3 hunches with 2500 participant answers collected per week. This will include new designs and test creation.
Weekly support and new feature design. Our cadence will include concept integration with engineering. Bugs will be resolved to improve the performance of the site.
Weekly analysis with website recommendations. All design work will be evaluated based on the lift. The tests will be prioritized based on the performance of the website.
